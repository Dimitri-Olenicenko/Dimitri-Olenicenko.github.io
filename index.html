Overview

who I am, in short

I build the systems institutions make decisions with — the data, the analysis, and increasingly the AI underneath them. Give me a ledger, a dataset or a market and I find what it is actually saying; then I turn that into a model, a report, or a product a board can act on. Numbers first, always: they are the one language every business speaks.

Eight years, four countries, 15+ industries — audit at Deloitte, enterprise BI in Sweden serving 40 controllers, PropTech and finance in Dubai, and three business lines today as Chief Data Officer at Naviora Group. Different sectors, one throughline: finding the causal thread in complexity and making it decision-ready. One method throughout: complexity in, clarity out.
