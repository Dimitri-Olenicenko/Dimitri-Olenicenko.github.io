Overview

who I am, in three honest paragraphs

Strategy, data and operations is the formal answer. The honest one: for eight years my job has been to walk into complexity and hand back structure. Regulatory requirements become specifications engineers can build from. Messy operational data becomes insight a board can decide on. An ambiguous initiative gets an owner, a sequence, and a finish line. I have done this across financial services, SaaS platform development and technology consulting, in four countries and three languages.

The range is deliberate: Deloitte audit rooms in Riga, three years inside the reporting engine of a Swedish enterprise, restaurant P&Ls in Dubai, a national statistics bureau, and now three business lines at Naviora Group. Different industries, same underlying work — finding the causal relationships between processes and structure, with one goal: the most efficient and fair way for everyone involved.

Beyond the CV, I am the architect of the New Era ecosystem: an institutional-grade investment operating system, a product studio, and the live platform neifcapital.com — designed and built end-to-end by one person, from data architecture and risk analytics to the running production system. The career and the projects tell one story: complexity in, clarity out.
